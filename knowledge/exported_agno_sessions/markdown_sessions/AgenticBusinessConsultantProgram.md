# IDEATION: Agentic business career accelerator program

Created: 7th April 2026

Let's design a new program for aspiring agentic business consultants.

Given the focus on an "agentic" approach and building on the successful, self-directed model we've discussed, we can create a powerful, hands-on program. The goal is to train consultants who don't just advise businesses but build and deploy AI-powered systems to solve their problems.

Here is a proposed high-level outline for a 12-week "Agentic Business Consultant Accelerator."

## **Program overview: The agentic business consultant accelerator**

This 12-week immersive program is designed to transform aspiring consultants into agentic business advisors. You will learn to leverage AI agents and automated workflows to analyze complex business challenges, design scalable systems, and deliver unparalleled value to clients.

This program is project-based, peer-to-peer, and self-directed. You will not just learn theory; you will build an actual agent-powered consulting practice from the ground up.

## **The program structure: three core projects**

The curriculum is built around three core projects that will take you from foundational knowledge to a full client simulation.

* **Project 1: The agentic consultant's toolkit.** You'll begin by building the foundations of your own consulting practice. This involves defining your niche and value proposition, creating a compelling service offering, and building your first "consultant agent" – an AI tool designed to automate a core consulting task, like market research or data analysis.
* **Project 2: The E-Myth automation challenge.** Drawing inspiration from "The E-Myth Revisited," you'll take a case study of a typical small business and design an agentic workflow to systematize a key area of its operations. This project will challenge you to think like a systems engineer, building a "crew" of AI agents that can manage a complex business process.
* **Project 3: The capstone client simulation.** In the final phase, you'll undertake a comprehensive client simulation. You'll be given a brief from a fictional company, and you'll be responsible for the entire engagement: scoping the project, deploying your agentic systems to conduct the analysis, and delivering a final presentation with actionable, data-driven recommendations.

## **The learning journey**

The 12-week accelerator is structured in three phases:

* **Phase 1: Foundations of agentic consulting (Weeks 1–4).** This phase focuses on the core principles of agentic business models and building your personal consulting toolkit.
* **Phase 2: Building your agentic practice (Weeks 5–8).** This phase is about practical application, as you dive deep into designing and building agentic workflows to solve real business problems.
* **Phase 3: The capstone client simulation (Weeks 9–12).** This final phase is a full-immersion experience, allowing you to apply all your skills in a realistic, high-stakes client engagement.
