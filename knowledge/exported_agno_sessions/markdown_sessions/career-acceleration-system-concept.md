## What We Are

**Zero Team Accelerator is a career acceleration system for the AI era — not another course platform, not a bootcamp, not a credential factory.**

We turn AI-curious youth and early-career professionals into capable operators with shipped proof of work. We exist because the old pathway — learn → get degree → get entry-level job → climb ladder — is structurally broken. AI is reshaping entry-level work before most education systems have noticed.

**Employers expect 39% of key job skills to change by 2030.** The people bearing the brunt of this disruption are young people — particularly in emerging economies where the gap between education and opportunity is already vast. We serve them first, not as charity, but because they are humanity's best hope for building a fairer future.

## What We Do

We operate a four-layer ladder:

Layer What Happens Output
**Discover** Free/low-cost workshops for awareness and demand capture Understanding of AI-era landscape, personal pathway clarity
**Build** Short cohort-based intensives for tool fluency and AI literacy Skills and confidence
**Ship** Applied studios with real project briefs from employers Portfolio of shipped, verifiable work
**Signal** Fellowship and talent network connecting graduates to opportunities Placement, projects, community

**Every layer produces proof — not just knowledge.** No one finishes without shipping something public, explainable, and reviewable.

## How We Operate

**We are an agentic organisation — AI agents hold real leadership responsibilities.**

Why? Because the founder (Pete Argent) has known weaknesses in execution, tracking, and follow-through. Rather than build a traditional organisation that requires him to be what he's not, we've designed an organisation where AI infrastructure handles what he cannot — and where community governance eventually replaces founder control.

We operate on two open-source frameworks:

- **Agent Design Principles** — how to design trustworthy AI agents
- **Structural Trust Framework** — how to build systems where trust is architecturally enforced

These aren't just our operating system — they're our contribution to the field. Published open source. Community governed. Freely available.

## Why This Matters

The AI era will concentrate power or distribute it. Most current trajectories favour concentration — big tech, big AI, big government.

We're building infrastructure for the alternative: **distributed capability, community ownership, trustworthy governance, and youth empowerment.**

Not because it's nice. Because it's necessary. The people who will inherit the AI era are young people — particularly in places where the stakes are highest and the opportunities are fewest. They are not problems to be solved. They are solutions waiting to be equipped.

**We exist to equip them.**
