Section 5

Governance Is the Defining Organisational Challenge

Most organisations are trying to solve an architecture problem with a documentation solution.

📋 Declarative Ethics
Ethics principles → oversight commitments → compliance checklists → training → culture. Works at small scale. Fails under pressure, at scale, across organisational boundaries, and in adversarial conditions.
Policy says "don't". It still happens.
🏗️ Structural Ethics
Encode constraints in the architecture so harmful actions are not merely discouraged but structurally impossible. An actor without the required capability cannot perform the action. Verification precedes acceptance.
Structure makes it impossible.

The Foundational Principle

"The quality of the contract
is the quality of the system."

When you give an agent a task, you are writing a contract. The agent executes it faithfully — whether the contract is good or not. The ability to specify intent precisely, define verification criteria, and constrain scope is the foundational skill that all other AI capability is built on.
