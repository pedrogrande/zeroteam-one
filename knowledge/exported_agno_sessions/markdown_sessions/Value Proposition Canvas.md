# Value Proposition Canvas

*Source: [Value Proposition Canvas – Download the Official Template](https://www.strategyzer.com/library/the-value-proposition-canvas)* 

(Answer these in relation to your service)

| Customer segment / Persona: |  |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Jobs to be done** What are the jobs your customer needs to get done in life (relative to your organisation)? Functional/Social/Emotional |  |
| **Gains** What makes your customer happy? What does your customer dream about? What would make their life easier? What do they want people to think about them? What do they hope they achieve? What give them feelings of pride and confidence? |  |
| **Pains** What is annoying to your customer? What stresses them? What upsets them? What keeps them up at night with worry? What do they fear people might think of them? What might they fear of not achieving? |  |
|  |  |
| **Products & Services** What products and/or services do you offer that help your clients get their ‘jobs’ done? |  |
| **Gain creators** What features of your product/service help your customers achieve their gains? Make their life easier?  Is special to your product/service?  |  |
| **Pain relievers** How can you help your customers relieve their pains? What problems can you eradicate?  How can you eliminate risks or fears?  How can you break down any barriers? |  |
