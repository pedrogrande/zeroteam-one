## How This Works: The Task Marketplace Model

Traditional consulting: 
- Partners bill clients at $300-500/hour
- Junior consultants do the work at $40-60/hour
- Partners keep the spread
- Juniors hope to "pay dues" for 7-10 years to become partners

**Our model:**

Every engagement is decomposed into **tasks with clear outputs**. Examples:
- "Conduct and transcribe 5 stakeholder interviews" 
- "Analyze activity logs to identify coordination overhead patterns"
- "Draft executive summary of findings"
- "Design workshop facilitation guide"

**You claim tasks based on:**
- Your current capabilities
- Your learning goals (want to try something new? Claim a stretch task)
- Your availability
- Your interests

**You earn based on:**
- Task completion and verification
- Quality of output
- Contribution value

**Typical split:**
- 40% of engagement revenue → task contributors
- 25% → engagement lead (owns client relationship and delivery)
- 20% → Future's Edge treasury (platform development, overhead)
- 15% → quality buffer (released after client acceptance)

**What this means for you:**

As an undergrad, you might earn AUD $800-2,000 from a single engagement doing research tasks that also become your portfolio pieces.

As an MBA grad, you might earn AUD $2,500-5,000 from an engagement where you lead research coordination and analysis—building consulting experience that normally requires 2 years at McKinsey to access.

As a mid-career professional, you might earn AUD $4,000-8,000 as an engagement lead while building evidence that you can run AI-augmented teams.

**And everyone learns by doing real work, not through hypothetical case studies.**
