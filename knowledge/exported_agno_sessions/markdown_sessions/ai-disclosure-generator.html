The five levels at a glance

The framework runs from Level 5 (entirely human-authored) to Level 1 (primarily AI-generated). Higher numbers = more human contribution.

Level 5Human-authored
No generative AI at any stage — including ideation, drafting, editing, or fact-checking. You conceived, structured, and wrote the entire piece yourself. Only traditional tools (spell-check, dictionaries) permitted.
Level 4AI-verified
You authored the work entirely. After completion, you used AI in a non-generative capacity — grammar checking, fact verification, or readability review. The AI did not generate content or suggest structural changes.
Level 3AI-assisted
The ideas, argument, and voice are yours. AI helped you refine phrasing, identify gaps, structure notes, or translate — but didn't originate the content. Remove the AI and the core work still exists in rougher form.
Level 2AI-collaborated
Genuine collaboration. AI generated substantial portions; you directed, challenged, curated, and shaped every iteration. Your intellectual contribution is real and significant — but the final product could not exist without the AI.
Level 1AI-generated with human curation
AI produced the primary content from a prompt or brief you provided. Your contribution was the quality of the brief, curation of the output, and judgment that it meets the required standard. Minimal post-generation editing.
