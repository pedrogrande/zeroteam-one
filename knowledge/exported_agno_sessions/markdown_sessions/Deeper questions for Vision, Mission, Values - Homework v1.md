# Vision, Mission, Values

---

### **Vision:**

1. **What is the organization known for in 5 years?**  
    *(Focuses on reputation and industry positioning.)*

2. **What values or principles define how the organization operates?**  
    *(Connects vision with guiding values.)*

3. **How is the organization adapting to industry trends or innovations?**  
    *(Encourages thinking about future-proofing the business.)*

4. **What long-term changes has the organization sparked in its industry or community?**  
    *(Explores transformational impact.)*

5. **How do people talk about the organization’s impact?**  
    *(Emphasizes storytelling and perception.)*

6. **What kind of partnerships or collaborations has the organization built?**  
    *(Highlights the importance of external relationships.)*

7. **What challenges in the world is the organization solving?**  
    *(Frames the vision in the context of global or societal issues.)*

8. **How does the organization inspire others to follow its example?**  
    *(Focuses on leadership and influence.)*

9. **What is the aspirational message the organization conveys to its audience?**  
    *(Identifies the emotional connection with stakeholders.)*

10. **What does the team culture look and feel like?**  
     *(Visualizes the internal dynamics and morale.)*

11. **What kind of leadership is steering the organization?**  
     *(Connects vision with leadership style and strategy.)*

---

### **Mission:**

1. **How is the organization’s approach unique or innovative?**  
    *(Explores differentiation in strategy or methods.)*

2. **What is the organization’s most significant strength?**  
    *(Focuses on its core competitive advantage.)*

3. **What does the organization consistently deliver better than anyone else?**  
    *(Helps identify excellence and specialization.)*

4. **How does the organization create value for its stakeholders?**  
    *(Connects purpose with tangible benefits.)*

5. **What long-term impact does the organization want to have on the world?**  
    *(Frames the mission in terms of enduring outcomes.)*

6. **What motivates the team to do this work?**  
    *(Explores intrinsic and collective motivation.)*

7. **What does success look like for the organization?**  
    *(Defines success from the organization’s perspective.)*

8. **How does the organization’s mission reflect the values of its founders or team?**  
    *(Aligns personal values with organizational goals.)*

9. **What commitments is the organization making to its audience or community?**  
    *(Defines promises and accountability.)*

10. **What are the most critical verbs or action words that describe what the organization does?**  
     *(Helps distill the mission into actionable terms.)*

---

### **Values:**

1. **How should the organization make difficult decisions?**  
    *(Defines principles for navigating challenges.)*

2. **What behaviors or qualities does the organization admire most in others?**  
    *(Clarifies aspirational traits.)*

3. **What values are most important when hiring or building a team?**  
    *(Focuses on team dynamics and culture-building.)*

4. **How does the organization encourage collaboration and teamwork?**  
    *(Explores strategies for fostering collaboration.)*

5. **What type of leadership does the organization value?**  
    *(Clarifies the leadership approach that aligns with values.)*

6. **What kind of relationship does the organization want with its customers?**  
    *(Explores values that guide customer interactions.)*

7. **What role does the organization play in its community or industry?**  
    *(Identifies how it contributes beyond its core work.)*

8. **What commitments does the organization make to its stakeholders?**  
    *(Defines accountability and trust-building efforts.)*

9. **What actions reflect the organization’s deepest values?**  
    *(Connects values with tangible behaviors and outcomes.)*

10. **What are three words that describe the organization’s personality?**  
     *(Helps distill values into memorable traits.)*

11. **What has shaped the founder's personal values, and how do these influence the organization?**  
     *(Explores personal history and its impact on values.)*

12. **What behaviors does the organization reward, and what does it discourage?**  
     *(Aligns values with recognition and accountability.)*

13. **How do these values align with the mission and vision?**  
     *(Ensures coherence across foundational elements.)*

14. **How can these values be explained in a way that is easy to remember and act upon?**  
     *(Focuses on practical application and communication.)*
