# Team ideation for Zero Team

Here is a recommended 30-person team designed specifically to launch and scale Zero Team as the commercial and consulting arm delivering AI literacy, structural trust frameworks, and agentic AI capability to organisations.

***

## Leadership & Strategy (3 roles)

These roles give Pete leverage — turning a solo-practitioner risk into an organisation.

- **Chief of Staff / Operations Lead** — *Goal: Build the operational backbone.* Turn the thought leadership strategy into a systemised, executable machine; own the content calendar, event pipeline, and commercial tracking dashboard so Pete's time goes to thinking and delivering, not administration.
- **Head of Commercial / Partnerships** — *Goal: Convert gravity into revenue.* Own the pipeline from inbound enquiry to signed engagement, develop the pricing architecture for workshops, advisory retainers, and Structural Trust Framework implementations, and build strategic alliances with ESA Victoria, RMIT, KPMG, and the University of Melbourne.
- **Head of Community & Ecosystem** — *Goal: Activate the ecosystem as a commercial moat.* Run Vibe Club, Good Vibes Only, and Future's Edge as interconnected talent and client pipelines; ensure the community layer feeds into Zero Team's proof assets and consulting funnel.

***

## Delivery & Consulting (5 roles)

These people deliver the core product and generate the proof assets that make the thought leadership credible.

- **Senior AI Governance Consultant (x2)** — *Goal: Deliver the Structural Trust Framework to 10+ client engagements.* Implement the four conformance profiles with organisations, document real outcomes, and produce the case studies that become Zero Team's most powerful credibility assets.
- **Learning Experience (LX) Designer** — *Goal: Productise the workshop portfolio.* Turn Pete's ESA-style workshop, the Good Vibes Only format, and any bespoke engagements into replicable, scalable learning products that can run without Pete in the room.
- **AI Literacy Facilitator / Trainer** — *Goal: Deliver 50+ workshop sessions.* Be the primary delivery vehicle for corporate AI literacy programs and public-sector engagements; owns participant satisfaction scores and post-workshop conversion rates.
- **Agentic AI Architect / Technical Lead** — *Goal: Build the technical credibility layer.* Develop and maintain demonstration-ready agentic AI systems that embody the Structural Trust Framework in practice — giving clients something tangible to see, not just read.

***

## Content & Thought Leadership (4 roles)

The noted weakness in Zero Team is "solo practitioner constraints" — these roles solve that.

- **Content Strategist / Managing Editor** — *Goal: Publish the full 12-month content calendar without dropping a deadline.* Own the Substack series execution, LinkedIn cadence, and framework document releases; ensures every piece meets the "one Zero Team concept carried away" quality standard.
- **Research Analyst** — *Goal: Keep Zero Team's evidence base ahead of the field.* Monitor emerging AI trust data (KPMG, McKinsey, OECD, PMC), identify the data points that make the strongest Signal posts, and support whitepaper and report writing.
- **Visual / Framework Designer** — *Goal: Make the IP unmistakable.* Create the trust stack diagram, multiplier framework one-pager, conformance profile ladder, and all shareable visuals; visual distinctiveness is a key part of how Zero Team's frameworks spread independently.
- **Video & Multimedia Producer** — *Goal: Extend reach beyond longform text.* Turn Substack articles, workshop sessions, and speaking appearances into short-form LinkedIn video, podcast episodes, and event highlight reels that surface Zero Team's thinking to audiences who don't read longform.

***

## Growth & Brand (3 roles)

Zero Team's brand recognition is currently nascent — these roles build the signal.

- **Growth & SEO Lead** — *Goal: Build Zero Team's inbound discovery engine.* Optimise the Substack and Zero Team website for the key search terms (structural trust, AI governance framework, AI literacy Australia), build the email list to 5,000 qualified subscribers by month 12.
- **PR & Media Relations Manager** — *Goal: Achieve first third-party media citation of Zero Team frameworks by October 2026.* Own relationships with tech journalists, policy editors, and podcast hosts; pitch the Trust Gap Report as a newsworthy document to AFR, The Australian, and ABC Tech.
- **Events & Partnerships Coordinator** — *Goal: Deliver 6+ major events in the first 12 months.* Execute Good Vibes Only, ESA Victoria workshops, the Vibe Club calendar, and speaking engagements; turn each event into a content asset and lead generation moment.

***

## Platform & Data (2 roles)

Zero Team's credibility depends on the quality of its own infrastructure.

- **Product / Platform Lead** — *Goal: Build the digital home for Zero Team's frameworks.* Own the website, Substack infrastructure, digital badge system (RMIT-credentialled), and any future course or certification platform; ensures everything is architected to convert visitors into clients.
- **Data & Impact Analyst** — *Goal: Quantify Zero Team's own trust dividend.* Build the measurement system for all three North Star signals — inbound enquiries citing frameworks, third-party citations, and speaking briefs that name the positioning — and produce monthly reporting.

***

## Mission Layer: Future's Edge (3 roles)

These roles keep the social mission engine running while Zero Team scales commercially.

- **Future's Edge Program Director** — *Goal: Deliver accredited youth AI literacy programs to 500+ young people (15–25).* Own program design, facilitator training, and impact measurement using the SROI framework from the Profitable Impact document.
- **Youth Partnerships Manager** — *Goal: Build 10 organisational partnerships.* Connect Future's Edge to schools, NFPs, government youth programs, and corporate CSR budgets; turns the mission layer into a revenue stream and proof asset simultaneously.
- **DAO & Digital Credentials Lead** — *Goal: Launch the blockchain-enabled credentialling system.* Implement the digital badge and DAO governance layer outlined in the Profitable Impact framework, giving young people verifiable, portable credentials that mean something in the labour market.

***

## Product Engineering (4 roles)

These four roles turn the intellectual IP — the Task Contract schema, the Structural Trust Framework's ONE model, the agentic workflow design tool — into real, shippable software.

- **Head of Product** — *Goal: Ship a public beta of the collaborative agentic workflow design tool by Q3 2026.* Own the full product roadmap across the tool suite, translate Zero Team's frameworks (Structural Trust, Task Contract schema, ONE model) into user-facing product decisions, and define the platform architecture that allows individual tools to interoperate as an emerging ecosystem.
- **Full-Stack Engineer (x2)** — *Goal: Build the core workflow design tool and its underlying data layer.* Implement the multi-layered Task Contract model, the Actor-agnostic architecture supporting both human and AI agents, the dependency graph engine, and the conformance profile system — the technical foundation everything else plugs into. One engineer leads backend (graph database, API, immutable audit trail); one leads frontend (the collaborative design interface).
- **AI/Agentic Systems Engineer** — *Goal: Implement live agent orchestration within the platform.* Build the execution layer where designed workflows are handed off to actual agentic systems (Dify, Agno, or equivalent), implement MCP tool boundary enforcement, and develop the executor-reviewer agent pairs that make structural trust verifiable in practice.

***

## Blockchain & Verifiability (2 roles)

The Structural Trust Framework already specifies an optional **Blockchain Trust Layer** that extends governance cryptographically across organisational boundaries. This is the team that builds it — and that connects it to Future's Edge's credentialling architecture.

- **Blockchain / Web3 Engineer** — *Goal: Build the Evidence Gate and blockchain trust layer.* Implement the cryptographic audit trail making governance artifacts independently verifiable by any party, develop the smart contract layer for Future's Edge's royalty and credentialling system, and prototype the DAO governance infrastructure that allows community treasury decisions to be structurally enforced rather than merely voted on.
- **Decentralised Identity & Credentials Architect** — *Goal: Launch verifiable digital credentials for the Zero Team ecosystem.* Design the portable, blockchain-anchored badge and credential system spanning Future's Edge youth programs, Vibe Club participation, and the Structural Trust Framework conformance certifications — creating a unified reputation layer that travels with people across organisations and platforms.

***

## How The Last 10 Connect to the First 20

These last 10 roles don't operate in isolation, they plug directly into the existing team:

- The **Agentic AI Architect** (original 20) becomes the bridge between the Research Lab's findings and the engineering team's implementation priorities
- The **Blockchain / Web3 Engineer** works directly with the **DAO & Digital Credentials Lead** (original 20) — one builds the infrastructure, one drives adoption
- The **Research Fellows** feed findings into the **Content Strategist** and **Research Analyst** (original 20), ensuring Zero Team's thought leadership remains grounded in original research rather than curated commentary
- The **Student Programs Coordinator** works hand-in-glove with the **Future's Edge Program Director** (original 20) — the lab is the grown-up version of what Future's Edge starts building in young people
