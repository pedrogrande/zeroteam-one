## What I'd Do (If I Were You)

1. **Name it.** Tell your students: "I'm building Zero Team Accelerator. It's for people like you. Some of you should be part of it."

2. **Design the invitation.** Not "sign up for my course" but "I'm building something for people who want to shape the AI era. I'm starting with a small group. Some of you are those people."

3. **Create the pathway.** Interested → Involved → Leading. What's the first step? Maybe: "Come to an info session. Then come to a pilot workshop. Then apply for the first cohort."

4. **Invite your unofficial community.** They're already inspired. Give them somewhere to go.

5. **Use your position deliberately.** You're a sessional lecturer — that's a feature, not a bug. You're not trapped in the institution. You're adjacent to it, with access, but not captured by it.
