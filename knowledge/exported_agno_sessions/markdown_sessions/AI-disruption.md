# EXPLORATION: AI Disruption

Created: 7th April 2026

---

## **1. Marketing & Creative Services**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Copywriting Agencies** → AI tools like Jasper, Copy.ai, GPT-powered platforms.
* **Graphic Design Studios** → Midjourney, DALL·E, Canva AI.
* **SEO Agencies** → AI-driven SEO optimization (e.g., Surfer SEO + ChatGPT workflows).
* **Ad Campaign Management** → Generative AI for creative + predictive AI for bidding (e.g., Pencil, AdCreative.ai).
* **Video Production & Editing** → AI video generators (e.g., Sora, Runway).

---

## **2. Professional Services**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Legal Services (SMEs, Contract Drafting, Reviews)** → Generative legal tools (e.g., Spellbook, Harvey).
* **Accounting & Bookkeeping Firms** → Automated AI-driven accounting (e.g., Pilot, Botkeeper, Vic.ai).
* **Business Consulting (Strategy/Analysis)** → AI research agents, business intelligence tools.
* **Market Research Firms** → AI-driven research synthesis and insight generators.
* **Recruitment Agencies (White Collar Roles)** → AI screening, CV parsing, talent matching (e.g., Manatal, HireEZ).

---

## **3. Customer Support & CX Agencies**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Outsourced Call Centers** → AI-powered chatbots, voice agents (e.g., Cognigy, PolyAI).
* **Customer Service Consulting** → AI-driven CX diagnostics and optimization.

---

## **4. IT & Software Development Services**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Website Development Studios (basic sites)** → AI web builders (e.g., 10Web, Wix AI, Framer).
* **App Development Shops (basic CRUD apps)** → AI code generation with Replit AI, GitHub Copilot, GPT-4.1 code agents.
* **Data Analytics Consulting** → AI dashboards and LLM-integrated BI tools (e.g., ThoughtSpot Sage).
* **Basic Cybersecurity Auditing** → AI-driven vulnerability scanning platforms.

---

## **5. HR, Training & Learning Services**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Employee Training Providers (Generic Programs)** → Personalized AI learning platforms (e.g., Sana, Learn.xyz).
* **Compliance Training** → AI-generated content tailored to company specifics.
* **HR Consulting for Policy Drafting** → AI-generated HR templates, policies.

---

## **6. Financial Services**

### Likely Disruptions

* **SMB-Focused Financial Advisory Firms** → AI-driven financial planning platforms.
* **SME Loan Brokers** → AI lending risk assessments and matching.
* **SMB Insurance Brokers** → AI-driven insurance matching and policy generation.

---

## **7. Translation & Localization Services**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Document Translation Firms** → GPT + Whisper + specialized LLMs outperform traditional translation.
* **Voiceover & Subtitling** → AI dubbing (e.g., HeyGen, ElevenLabs, Rask.ai).

---

## **8. Event & PR Agencies**

### Likely Disruptions

* **PR Writing & Distribution Firms** → AI-generated press releases.
* **Event Marketing Firms** → AI for guest targeting and engagement automation.

---

## **9. Architecture & Engineering Support**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Drafting & Rendering Services** → AI-assisted CAD tools and generative design.
* **3D Modeling Services** → Generative 3D (e.g., Luma AI, Kaedim).

---

## **10. Virtual Assistant Agencies**

### Likely Disruptions

* **Administrative VA Firms** → AI agents acting as executive assistants (e.g., Personal AI, Augment, Adept).
* **Scheduling/Email Management VAs** → AI-native email and calendar management.

---

## **Sectors Less Likely to Face Immediate Disruption:**

* Specialized advisory firms (where nuance, human trust, and context matter).
* Relationship-driven businesses (e.g., boutique investment banks, executive-level strategy consulting).
* Bespoke creative agencies for high-value, brand-defining work.
