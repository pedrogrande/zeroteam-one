# Trustworthy by Design: The Practitioner's Handbook for Human-Centred Agentic Systems

*Positioning*: A decision-support tool, not a sequential read. Every chapter opens with the decision it supports and closes with a practical instrument the reader can use immediately. The handbook sits alongside your work, not on a shelf. 

***

## How to Use This Handbook

A dedicated opening section maps four reader pathways — allowing readers to navigate directly to what matters most without reading cover to cover:

| Reader Type | Primary Path | Core Chapters |
|---|---|---|
| **Business Leader** | Strategic case → Governance → Economics | Parts I, V, VI, VIII |
| **System Designer** | Architecture → Agent Design → Implementation | Parts II, III, IV, VII |
| **Governance Professional** | Trust → Compliance → Evaluation | Parts II, V, VI |
| **Full Practitioner** | Sequential start to finish | All parts |

Each chapter is also tagged with its primary audience (🏢 Leader / 🔧 Designer / ⚖️ Governance) so readers always know where they are.

## Handbook Back Matter

Three appendices that transform the handbook from a read into a permanent reference tool:

- **Appendix A** — STF Quick Reference Card: all 24 principles on two pages with conformance signals
- **Appendix B** — ONE Ontology Reference: Thing types, Connection types, and Event primitives in a lookup format
- **Appendix C** — Regulatory Crosswalk: STF principles mapped to ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, and EU AI Act articles
- **Glossary** — 40–50 defined terms, keeping language consistent across the business-leader and designer audiences

## What Changed from the Book Concept

- Every chapter now delivers a **named practical instrument** — the reader leaves each chapter with something usable, not just something understood 
- **Reader pathways** replace the assumption of sequential reading — a governance professional can open Part V without reading Parts II–IV first
- **Audience tags** per chapter signal relevance without forcing readers to scan every section
- Part titles are **outcome-oriented** rather than conceptual
- Three new **appendices** make the book a long-term reference rather than a one-time read
