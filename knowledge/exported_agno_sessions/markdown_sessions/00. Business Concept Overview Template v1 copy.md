# Business Concept Overview

This template provides a structured framework for outlining your business concept and addressing key questions that are crucial for strategic planning and decision-making.

| Key Questions | Your Response |
| :---- | :---- |
| Can you provide an overview of your business concept or idea? (What is the business about, and what problem does it solve?) |  |
| Who are your target customers or key stakeholders? (Demographics, locations, and needs.) |  |
| What is your business model? (How does your business generate revenue?) |  |
| Which markets or regions are you planning to operate in? (Domestic, international, or online?) |  |
| Who are your main competitors, and what differentiates you from them? |  |
| What are your short-term and long-term goals for the business? |  |
| Are there any major risks or uncertainties you’re currently worried about? |  |
| What partnerships, suppliers, or stakeholders are critical to your success? |  |
| What is your vision for the business’s impact on its industry or community? |  |
